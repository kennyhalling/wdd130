Overview

Purpose

We want to create a site that caters to all types of rafters; whether they are just beginning or experienced, we want them to be able to quickly find what resources they need with a quick look at our site. In doing this, we hope to retain our loyal customers and attract new ones wishing to explore white water rafting.

Audience

Our audience will primarily consist of the 18 to 30 demographic, both experienced and not, who are interested in outdoor sports. We want to quickly show them how much our services cost based on what they expect, and to provide them with easy to access information surrounding what service they choose. We hope to surpass our competitors by providing our audience with quick-access information and a wide variety of services that apply to those looking to budget or those looking to splurge. In a world where phones are a growing necessity at everyone's side, we want to focus on making our site accesible primarily to mobile devices.
