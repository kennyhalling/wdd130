Notable Experience

Throughout my life thus far, I have achieved a few different things worth noting outside of normal work experience:

Throughout my time in middle school and high school I joined the Boy Scouts of America and managed to obtain the rank of Eagle Scout at the age of 17. In high school, I took the initiative of enrolling in Advanced Placement courses and even some summer courses at Austin Community College to open up my schedule for other classes in my junior and senior years. Among all my achievements outside of work, however, my biggest was probably successfully serving two years as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints in Porto Alegre, Brazil and various parts of West Virginia during the COVID-19 pandemic. During my time in service, I learned how to hold a conversation in Portuguese and how to successfully teach. I spent a lot of time at various food pantries serving the community in West Virginia.
